Tune World

A challenge to myself to learn how to program an android app in three days. I have always had a passion for music so I created this music maker. I played viola in school so the notes used on the app are all viola. Every tap to the screen produces a new note and changes the background color. The app also includes a tuner that plays the classic A to tune instuments with.
